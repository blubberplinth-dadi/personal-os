# Taking PM Ownership of Bóthildur (and Claims)

## Why this project exists

Daði transitions from Kolibri contractor to direct TM employee on May 1, 2026. This project captures what taking full PM ownership of Bóthildur and claims looks like — responsibilities, relationships, and transition steps.

## Goal alignment

Supports Personal Development Goal 1 (Build PM skills) and is foundational to delivering on both 2026 Objectives.

## Key questions to answer

- What does PM ownership of Bóthildur and claims mean in practice?
- What responsibilities shift on May 1 vs. what Daði already does today?
- What relationships or agreements need to be established or formalized?
- What needs to happen before May 1, and what can happen after?
