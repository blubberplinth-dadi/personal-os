# Why Empowered Product Teams

## Goal

Engage LB IT leadership about shifting from matrix management + task forces to empowered product teams. Build the case, get buy-in, and propose a path forward.

## Context

LB IT currently organizes work through matrix management and task forces. This creates challenges for ownership, speed, and accountability — especially as TM's digital products require sustained, cross-functional effort. Daði has direct experience with product teams through Kolibri and wants to champion this shift.

**Audience:** LB IT leadership (Arinbjörn, Óskar S, and others)

**Ties to goals:**
- Personal Dev Goal 1: Build PM skills
- Adjacent ownership: improving how TM/LB teams deliver digital products

## Key Questions

- What's LB's current pain with the matrix/task force model?
- What does "empowered product team" mean in the LB/TM context specifically?
- Who are the allies and skeptics?
- What's a realistic first step — full reorg or a pilot?
