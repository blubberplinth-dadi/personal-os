# TM

## Company Background

**Founded:** 1956
**Headquarters:** Reykjavik, Iceland
**Industry:** Insurance
**Product lines:** Auto, Property and casualty, Life, Health - both for individuals and corporations
**Employees:** 120 people
**Parent company:** Landsbankinn (LB for short) - acquired TM in early spring 2025. Insurance business remains separate corporation under bank control.

**Mission:** Help people ensure their financial future.

## Core Digital Products (owned by TM)

**Græna tryggingakerfið** — AS/400 terminal-based insurance and customer management system from the 1980s. Still the go-to system for most employees. Used for corporate customers while Rósin handles individuals.

**Græna tjónakerfið** — AS/400 terminal-based claims management system from the 1990s. Still the go-to system for most employees for claims work.

**Bóthildur** — New web-based claims management system. Still in development and not yet the default claims system.

**Rósin** — 11-year-old web-based insurance and customer management system. Used for individual customers (corporate customers remain in Græna tryggingakerfið).

**Heimdallur** — Centralized access control system for managing user permissions across TM's insurance systems (Bóthildur, Rósin, Vefsala, etc.). Still in development — not all systems have been integrated yet.

## Digital Products Transitioning to LB

**Vefsalan** — Portal for individual customers to purchase insurance. Originally developed by TM, now being moved to LB.

**Mínar síður** — Customer portal. Originally developed by TM, now being moved to LB.

## Relationship with LB

**Acquisition:** LB acquired TM in early spring 2025. TM remains a separate corporation — the insurance business operates independently but shares IT resources with LB.

**Org structure overlap:**
- TM's digital products are built by the Tryggingalausnir group within LB IT (led by Óli), not by TM's own developers
- Product management sits at TM (Óskar Örn as CPO, Daði as PM), engineering sits at LB

**Product ownership:**
- **TM-owned:** Bóthildur, Rósin, Heimdallur, Græna tryggingakerfið, Græna tjónakerfið
- **Transitioning to LB:** Vefsala, Mínar síður — originally developed by TM, now moving to LB ownership
